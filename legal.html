To the people of Ikaram and other Abesabesi communities

The Abesabesi grammar and dictionary tries to describe the language spoken in Ikaram and the other Abesabesi-speaking communities accurately. If you feel that your community, particular people, or the language are represented in a wrong or unfavorable way, please contact Jonas Lau (contact information on the right hand side), and we will find a solution to correct or delete information in order to achieve a better representation of your community. Please contact me also for feedback, if you detected mistakes, or if you'd like to contribute further information to this site.

Legal Notice - English

The Abesabesi Grammar is an application that serves at the illustration and dissemination of research findings produced in Jonas Lau's (2021) dissertation "A Digital Reference Grammar of Abesabesi". It contains, among others, parts of the Abesabesi Grammar Sketch, which was first published in the dissertation, and an English-Abesabesi dictionary. No responsibility is taken for the correctness and currentness of the information. Despite careful examination, no responsibility is taken for the content of external links.
