How to use the dictionary

You can use the dictionary to look up lexical entries. In the center, below the picture, you can find a search field to look up entries by typing the Abesabesi word form or the English gloss. The searched entry will be shown below the search field with all its details. On the right side, you can find a complete list of all entries. The table can be sorted by clicking on the column heads.
